I'm Ryan Blecher and I'm a 29 year old developer in training. I'm currently enrolled at DevBootCamp in NYC and I'm going through phase-0. Right now I'm working on my html/css skills, so bear with me as i try to make my site look pretty on the inside and out!

A few years ago I started fixing guitar and bass amplifiers for friends and fell in love with "being close to the metal". I eventually became very serious about educating myself and started my own business. This coincidentally rekindled my love affair with code. I had dabbled in coding html/c++ for years, but never got anywhere, mostly due to my lack of direction. Then this year I took a class in Python programming which just screamed at me that I needed to code.

Now I'm currently pursuing my technolust professionally and I'm more excited than ever to meet and study with a myriad of like-minded nerd-folk. Being able to create something even as simple and bare-bones as this gitHub profile site has given me immense joy! Please feel free to look around and contact me with any questions or comments.
